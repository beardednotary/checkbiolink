Frequently Asked Questions

What actually causes links to break?

The most common causes are expired SSL certificates (your site shows "Not Secure"), domain registration lapses (you forgot to renew), DNS configuration errors, hosting server failures, and platform bans or blocks (Instagram or TikTok flagging certain URL shorteners). Any of these can take your links offline without warning.

How often do you check my links?

It depends on your plan. Starter checks every 4 hours (6 times per day), Pro checks every 2 hours (12 times per day), and Agency checks every hour (24 times per day). The more critical your links, the more frequent monitoring you'll want.

What happens the moment a link goes down?

You get an instant email alert with the link that broke, the exact error (timeout, 404, SSL failure, etc.), and when it was detected. No technical jargon - just clear information so you can fix it fast.

Do you monitor Linktree, Beacons, and Shopify?

Yes. CheckBioLink monitors any URL you give us - Linktree, Beacons, Shopify stores, Etsy shops, Calendly booking pages, WordPress sites, custom domains, and more. If it has a URL, we can monitor it.

Can I monitor links on multiple platforms at once?

Absolutely. Monitor your Etsy shop, Instagram bio link, Calendly booking page, and website all from one dashboard. Perfect for creators and small business owners who drive traffic from multiple sources.

What if I don't have a technical background?

CheckBioLink requires zero technical setup. Just paste your URLs and we handle the rest. You'll get plain-English alerts telling you exactly what broke and when - no server logs or cryptic error codes.

Is there a free trial?

Yes - 14 days free, no credit card required. Add your links and see how it works before paying anything. If it's not for you, just walk away. No strings attached.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel with one click from your dashboard. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no hoops to jump through. You're in control.

What's the difference between CheckBioLink and free uptime monitors?

Most free tools only check once per hour (or less) and send cryptic technical alerts full of server logs. We check as often as every hour on our Agency plan, and our alerts are built for non-technical users - you get clear, actionable information in plain English.

Do you store or track my customer data?

No. We only check if your link is up or down. We don't access your analytics, customer information, or any private data from your sites. Your privacy and your customers' privacy are protected.
