Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you check my links?

Check frequency depends on your plan: Starter checks every 4 hours (6x daily), Pro checks every 2 hours (12x daily), and Business checks every hour (24x daily).

What happens when my link is down?

You'll receive an immediate alert via email (or SMS on Pro/Business plans) with specific details about the error, when it was detected, and which link is affected.

Why should I monitor my bio link?

Your bio link is often the only clickable link on platforms like Instagram and TikTok. When it breaks, you lose 100% of your traffic. Many creators don't realize their link is down until days later, costing hundreds or thousands in lost revenue.

What causes links to break?

Links break for many reasons: domain renewals lapse, hosting services have outages, link shorteners experience downtime, redirects get misconfigured, or SSL certificates expire. Even major platforms like Linktree occasionally have outages.

What types of links can I monitor?

You can monitor any URL: direct social media bio links, Linktree/Beacons pages, affiliate links, course pages, booking systems, lead magnets, custom domains, or any other link critical to your business.

Which platforms do you support?

We support links from any platform including Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, and any other social network. We also monitor all major link-in-bio tools like Linktree, Beacons, and Tap Bio.

How is this different from checking myself?

Manual checking is inconsistent, time-consuming, and easy to forget. Most creators only discover problems when followers report it or they randomly check days later. Automatic monitoring gives you peace of mind and immediate alerts 24/7.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When you downgrade, your new rate starts at the next billing cycle.
